How to Use Auhydis

Simple steps to get your hygiene essentials!

Follow these easy steps:

  • Access Our Menu:
    • Scan the QR code displayed on the vending machine using your smartphone.
    • Alternatively, you can use the tablet provided at the table/counter to browse products.

    This will take you to our website: auhydis.com

  • Select Your Products:
    • On the website, choose the "View Machine" option for the machine in front of you.
    • Browse the available items, select the quantity you need, and add them to your cart.
  • Proceed to Checkout:
    • Once you've added all desired items to your cart, click the "Checkout" button.
    • Review your order summary on the checkout page.
